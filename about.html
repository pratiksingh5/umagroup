The group ventured, in the year 1962, into metal fabrication with the manufacturing of various metal parts, components and articles. It thereafter selected the path of manufacturing exclusive quality products for the lighting industry. Uma Group has interests in LED lighting, Plastic moulded & Wooden furniture, End-to End sitting arrangement for largest stadiums in India, pressure die casting, mould tool room, and sheet metal fabrication with manufacturing facilities at Kolkata, Nagpur and Guwahati.
